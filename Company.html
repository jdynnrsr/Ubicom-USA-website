COMPANY PROFILE

We started in 1993 as an Offshore Research and Development arm of a joint venture between IBM Japan and Toshiba TEC, we later on became an independent company, establishing our head office in Japan.

With more than 25 years in the business operating in the Philippines, Japan, China, and United States, we are the strategic partner of choice by renowned companies like IBM Japan, Ltd., Lenovo, Kyocera, Sumitomo Heavy Industries, and Toshiba-TEC Corporation.

Our teams of engineers are both technical experts and bi-lingual (English and Japanese), providing software development solutions and services.

Let us help you navigate and thrive in the increasingly changing world of technology through strategic consulting, operational leadership, focus on excellence, and the co-creation of strategic breakthrough solutions.

MESSAGE OF THE PRESIDENT

Tadashi Ishikawa

President

Continue to challenge the world with a bright and positive smile.

AWS (Advanced World Systems, Inc., the predecessor of Ubicom Group, was founded in 1993. and has built a solid business foundation based on our 25-year experience in R&D and IT personnel development in the Philippines.

We see social structural changes such as internationalization, the declining birthrate and aging population, and social innovations such as technological innovation in the fields of medical life sciences and robots and AI as opportunities for creating new businesses.

In addition to the existing manufacturing and service industries, the group is working together to develop and provide unique solution services utilizing state-of-the-art technologies in the financial, medical, automotive and robotics fields.

In the future, we will aim to further improve our corporate value by expanding our group through M&A and capital and business alliances and by promoting our unique investment Win・Win model that leverages the advantages ofour domestic competitors

Finally, the group's strength is to position its employees and their families, including overseas, as "friends", and by keeping everyone smiling and always cheerful and positive, each person is ahead of the times with ownership without being satisfied with the current situation. It is a corporate group that makes leaps and bounds.

We will continue to create new markets as a company that brings together young and motivated people from Asian countries to bring together the most creative people in the industry and talk about their dreams.
